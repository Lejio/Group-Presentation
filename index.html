The Construction Problem

There is a massive construction issue on campus. As University of Maryland students ourselves, we've all experienced the disruption of construction on campus.

The Construction Problem

Given the major construction projects taking place on the University of Maryland campus, there should be an effort made to reduce the disruption. Whether it is to splitting up a construction project, creating a construction free walkway, or notifying students of construction delays, something must be done.
